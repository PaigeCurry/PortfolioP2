About Me

About My Work

I am a character designer and digital illustrator who enjoys creating characters and telling stories through visuals. My work focuses on emotion, personality, and mood. Through design, color, and composition, I try to communicate who a character is and the world they belong to.

Many of my pieces experiment with stylization and lighting. I enjoy exploring different artistic approaches and incorporating fantasy influences into my work. This allows me to create characters that feel unique while still grounded.

My portfolio includes character concepts, finished illustrations, and experimental work created throughout my development as an artist. This website includes both my featured portfolio pieces and an archive of additional studies and projects that show my growth, creative process, and art style.
